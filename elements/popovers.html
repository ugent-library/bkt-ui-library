Popovers

Bootstrap popovers, initialised globally by popovers.js — any element with data-bs-toggle="popover" works, including inside HTMX-swapped fragments. Triggers inside links need data-bs-container="body", or the popover becomes part of the click target.

Variants

Default — click to toggle
Small — short metadata, identifier chips
Dark — tooltip-like emphasis on light backgrounds
Small + dark, hover trigger — identifier icons in author lists (public work detail)

Accessibility

Hover-triggered popovers are invisible to keyboard and screen reader users. The information must also exist in the DOM — the author-list icons pair each popover with a visually-hidden span carrying the same ID.
