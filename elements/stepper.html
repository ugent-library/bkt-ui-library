Stepper

Progress indicators for multi-step workflows. Used in deposit flows to show current position and completed steps. Each step is a link pointing to its section.

Accessibility

  • Wrap in <nav> with descriptive aria-label
  • Use aria-current="step" on the current step (not aria-current="page")
  • Completed steps get aria-current="false" to indicate they're not current
  • Each step is a link (<a href>) that navigates to its section
  • Screen readers can navigate the stepper as a navigation landmark
  • Step numbers are announced as part of the link text
