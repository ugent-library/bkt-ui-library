Urban forests as essential infrastructure for climate resilience and biodiversity: a call to policymakers

Esperon‑Rodriguez, M., Arndt, S., De Pauw, K. Loading…

Plants People Planet · Vol. 8, No. 1 · 2026 · pp. 14–19

Download PDF

Abstract

By 2050, nearly 70% of the global population will live in cities. Urban forests form the backbone of green infrastructure supporting resilient, equitable, and sustainable cities.

Urban areas are facing increasing risks from climate change, including heatwaves, flooding, wildfires, and growing social inequality, which challenges urban planning and design.
