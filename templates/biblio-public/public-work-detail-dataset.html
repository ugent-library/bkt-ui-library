Description

Tree inventory, canopy cover, and continuous microclimate measurements for 42 urban forest plots across Ghent, collected between 2020 and 2025.

Includes plot-level species composition, air and soil temperature series, and derived heat-mitigation indicators used in the accompanying journal article.
