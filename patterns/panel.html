Panel

A generic popover panel — a titled header, a scrollable body, and an actions footer — shown inside a dropdown or overlay. It sizes to its content. The filter editor is a wider (480px) specialisation of the same chrome.

Anatomy

bt-panel wraps three parts: bt-panel__title, a bt-panel__body (add bt-panel__body--checklist to stack option rows; the body scrolls past ~240px), and a bt-panel__actions footer. Option rows are plain Bootstrap form-check. Live use: the add-to-list picker on public-works.

In a dropdown

On result cards the panel is the menu of a Bootstrap dropdown: put bt-panel on the .dropdown-menu with p-0, and give the toggle data-bs-auto-close="outside" so toggling checkboxes doesn't close it. HTMX drives membership (hx-put / hx-delete) and create (hx-post), mirroring raven's AddToListComponent.
