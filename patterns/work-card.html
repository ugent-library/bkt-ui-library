Patterns

Work card

The primary unit for displaying research output in list and search contexts. Three variants: backoffice curator, backoffice researcher, and public. The card uses Bootstrap's card component as a structural base, with surface tokens and a small set of custom classes for domain-specific content.

Backoffice — curator

All metadata visible: status, authors, affiliations, departments, projects, Biblio message, audit trail, VABB, quick links.
