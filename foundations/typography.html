Foundations

Typography

IBM Plex Serif for editorial weight — titles, abstracts, long-form reading. IBM Plex Sans for interface clarity. IBM Plex Mono for identifiers, code, and technical strings. All three designed as a system by Mike Abbink at IBM. Open source, self-hostable.
